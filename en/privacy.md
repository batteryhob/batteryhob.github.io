# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 19, 2025**

Battery Ho Tech Blog ("this website") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

## 1. Information We Collect

This website may collect the following types of information:

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Visit Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system, visit date and time
- **Usage Information**: Pages visited, time spent on pages, referral sources
- **Device Information**: Device type, screen resolution

### Cookies
This website uses cookies for service improvement and statistical analysis. Cookies are small text files stored by your browser. You can refuse cookies through your browser settings.

## 2. Purpose of Information Collection

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

- **Service Provision**: Providing website content and improving functionality
- **Statistical Analysis**: Analyzing visitor statistics and usage patterns for service improvement
- **Advertising Services**: Providing personalized ads through Google AdSense

## 3. Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services:

### Google Analytics
This website uses Google Analytics for traffic analysis. Google Analytics collects anonymized visitor information through cookies. For more details, please refer to [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Google AdSense
This website uses Google AdSense for advertising. Google AdSense may use cookies to display ads based on your interests. You can disable personalized ads in [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

### GitHub Pages
This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. For information about GitHub's data collection and processing, please refer to [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement).

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion

This website deletes personal information without delay after the purpose of collection and use has been achieved. However, information may be retained for the period required by applicable laws and regulations.

## 5. Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights:

- **Refuse Cookies**: You can refuse cookie usage through your browser settings
- **Disable Personalized Ads**: You can disable personalized ads in Google Ads Settings
- **Information Requests**: You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of collected personal information
