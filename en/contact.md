## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I share your blog posts?
**A:** Yes, you're free to share for non-commercial purposes. Please include the source (blog URL) and if you modify the content, share under the same license. (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0)

### Q: How do I inquire about advertising or sponsorship?
**A:** Please contact me via email, and I'll review and respond. I consider collaborations limited to technology-related products or services.

### Q: Do you offer technical consulting?
**A:** For consulting on MLOps, data engineering, or cloud infrastructure, please reach out via email. We can discuss based on project scope and timeline.

### Q: I have a topic I'd like you to cover
**A:** Great suggestion! Send me the topic via email, and I'll try to cover it if it's within my expertise.

### Q: The code isn't working
**A:** If a code example from the blog isn't working, please send me an email with your environment details (OS, versions, etc.) and the error message, and I'll look into it.

## Response Time

- **General inquiries**: Response within 2-3 days
- **Urgent inquiries**: ASAP (mark subject with [URGENT])
- **Collaboration proposals**: Review and response within 1 week

## Privacy

Personal information collected during contact (email address, etc.) is used only for responding to your inquiry and is safely deleted after completion. For more details, please see our [Privacy Policy](/en/privacy/).
